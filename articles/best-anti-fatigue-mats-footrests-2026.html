Footrest or mat? Different tools for different modes: An anti-fatigue mat supports you while standing. An under-desk footrest supports you while sitting. If your standing desk is your primary workstation, you need both — the mat for standing sessions, the footrest to maintain seated posture when your chair is at standing-desk height.

06 // Mats vs Footrests: What You Actually Need

If you stand for more than two hours daily, buy an anti-fatigue mat first — the ComfiLife at $35–$45 is the no-regret entry point, the Topo at $99 is the long-term upgrade. If your feet dangle when seated (common for anyone under 5'8"), a footrest is the higher priority because poor seated posture causes back pain faster than standing on a hard floor. Budget $70–$120 for both if you are building a full-time battlestation from scratch.
